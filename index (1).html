Our Story

A family tradition of
flawless craft

Founded in 2008 by master cleaner Robert Ashford, Prestige Clean began as a small atelier committed to one simple principle: treat every garment as if it were your own. Fifteen years later, that philosophy guides everything we do — from the solvents we select to the way we fold and package your clothes.

We've served thousands of clients across the city, from busy professionals to bridal parties, from fashion designers to families — all trusting us with their most valued pieces.
